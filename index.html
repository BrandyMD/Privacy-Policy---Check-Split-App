Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 08, 2026 | Application: Check Split

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application Check Split ("Application"). The Application is a clean, utility tool engineered to compute rapid proportional bill splits and individual seat breakdowns locally on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data: The Application respects user anonymity. It does not require user accounts, registrations, profiles, or network sign-ins. We do not directly collect, harvest, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses. All mathematical bill configurations stay securely processed within temporary local memory variables.

3. User Preferences & Control

You hold complete command over individual tracking footprints. You can instantly reset or disable ad personalization parameters globally by visiting your local device system configuration dashboard (Settings > Privacy > Ads on native Android frameworks).

The Application does not intentionally collect or evaluate tracking analytics targeting users under the age of 13. If a legal guardian determines an automated tracking token was stored from a protected minor's device, reach out to ensure proper exclusion steps are completed.
